Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 50g parmesan
  • 2 plump garlic cloves
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • Sea salt and freshly ground black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Put a large saucepan of water on to boil.
  2. Finely chop the pancetta, having first removed any rind.
  3. Beat the eggs in a medium bowl, season with a little freshly grated black pepper and set everything aside.
  4. Add the spaghetti to the water and cook according to the packet instructions.
  5. Squash the garlic with the blade of a knife, just to bruise it.
  6. Melt the butter in a large frying pan and, when it starts to foam, add the pancetta and garlic.
  7. Keep the heat hot until the pancetta is golden and crisp.
  8. Remove the garlic and take the pan off the heat.
  9. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the pan with the pancetta.
  10. Mix most of the cheese in with the eggs, keeping a small handful back for sprinkling over later.
  11. Quickly pour the eggs and cheese into the pasta, lifting up the spaghetti so it mixes easily.
  12. Serve immediately with a little sprinkling of the remaining cheese and a grating of black pepper.
